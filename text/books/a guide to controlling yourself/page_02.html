Basic Controls

Controls can be changed in the top right under settings, macros.

Mouse1: Use Right Hand
Mouse2: Use Left Hand
W,A,S,D: Move.
Shift: Sprint
Alt: Alternative Action*
Ctrl: Grab*
R: Throw*
Q: Drop*
C: Hold (Dodge/Block/Parry)
B: Toggle Sneak
V: Toggle Zoom
E: Examine Mode*
Z: Quick Self*
1: Intent Help
2: Intent Disarm
3: Intent Grab
4: Intent Harm
G: Cycle Intent Clockwise
F: Cycle Intent Counter-Clockwise
T: Say
O: OOC
L: LOOC
* Indicates that this must be combined with mouse clicking in order to work.
